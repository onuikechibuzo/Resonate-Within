What we do

I’m here to help heal hearts

Individual Intuitive Healing

You and I work together to reveal the issue/concern as I channel messages from your guides. During this process specific energy releasing techniques are utilized so that a positive resolution can be achieved. This approach gets to the ‘heart of the matter’ in order to alleviate stress and discomfort on all levels.

Sound Healing Session

Sound (vibrational) healing techniques are used to facilitate healing following a discussion related to client needs and wants. The use of sound tools offers up healing opportunities on levels that often cannot be easily measured, however, individuals typically walk away feeling an energetic shift.

Family Constellation Work

Principles related to the Orders of Love (Bert Hellinger’s work) are used to restore symmetry in a family system. Issues such as lack of closeness with parent(s), limiting beliefs, illness, fears, sibling disharmony, being left out, etc. can be addressed within this format. This is a very interactive process that can be done virtually. Ask me for more details about this extremely PROFOUND and life altering work.

Sacred Art Healing Program

This 6-week virtual course uses sacred art to support emotional healing, offering an alternative to verbal expression. The first session focuses on identifying emotions that need healing, with each week tailored to the individual's needs. The sessions are guided by Divinity, and their content may evolve during the process.

Infant/Newborn Trauma Cleansing

Energetic cleansing and blessing of infants and newborns who may have experienced birth trauma (i.e. breach birth, long labor, forcept delivery, ‘stuck’ in birth canal, etc) Reach out for details related to this service.

Speech Therapy

Speech therapy services for adults and children offered virtually. This is a holistic approach to working with and addressing speech, language and swallowing disorders.

Shamanic Cleansing

Energetic cleansing of one’s home, land, person and/or pet offered virtually.

Sacred Art Healing Program

During this program we will meet 1x/week for 6-weeks. Each session will be about 1-1.5 hours long. Making art helps us discover who we are and should be available to everyone. You do not need worry about buying art supplies- you can work with nature to make the most wonderful art. Program pricing is $333.
